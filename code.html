Our Heritage: From Vaidya Heritage to Rahat Unani

Rooted in the historical heart of Bhiwandi, Rahat Unani carries forward the legacy formerly known as Vaidya Heritage. For generations, we have served the community with authentic Greco-Arabic medicinal wisdom.

Our transition to Rahat Unani represents a modern commitment to these ancient practices. We ensure every herb, spice, and dry fruit is ethically sourced and scientifically verified, maintaining the purity our ancestors trusted.
